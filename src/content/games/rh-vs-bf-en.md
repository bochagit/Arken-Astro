**Arken Games** brings this epic clash between legends: the cunning **Robin Hood** against the colossal **Bigfoot**, in a 1v1 asymmetric battle packed with tactics, fun, and miniatures.

## - Heroes and Allies -

**Robin Hood** leads his four **banditos**, excelling at ranged attacks and with abilities that let him strike and then quickly retreat, leveraging terrain to stay out of harm's way.

**Bigfoot**, armed with great mobility (3 movement) and accompanied by his loyal **Mothperson**, is a true force of nature: he can move through enemies and, if left alone in his zone at turn's end, draws extra cards.

## - Mechanics and Gameplay Experience -

Combat revolves around efficient hand management: do you use cards for movement, attack, or defense? Each turn you can perform two actions among maneuvering, attacking, or executing special plays. Robin Hood's tactics let him attack from range and reposition with agility, while Bigfoot imposes his presence with brute force and advantages when controlling terrain.

## - Why Choose Unmatched: Robin Hood vs. BigFoot? -

Because it's an **excellent entry point to the Unmatched system** for 2 players, with accessible rules and heroes with vastly different playstyles, making every game fresh and entertaining.

Because it includes **two exclusive maps** (Sherwood Forest and Yukon), which not only offer visual variety but also new strategic dynamics depending on the terrain.

**Because it expands and strengthens your Unmatched collection:** this set is fully compatible with any other title in the line. You can pit Bigfoot against King Arthur, or Robin Hood against Medusa. Each character brings a unique deck with its own mechanics, multiplying combinations and the system's replayability. Furthermore, by incorporating characters with such opposite styles—one agile and tactical, the other powerful and direct—this set becomes a great tool for teaching the game to new players or for varying your regular games.
