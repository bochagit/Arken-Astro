**Arken Games** presents the first edition of *Unmatched*, an asymmetric miniatures and card game for 2 to 4 players, where legendary heroes from different universes face off in unique and dynamic combat.

## - Heroes and Allies -

**King Arthur** can sacrifice cards to power up his attacks and is supported by the wizard **Merlin**.

**Alice**, with her vorpal sword, changes size to adapt to offense or defense, accompanied by the fearsome **Jabberwock**.

**Medusa** dominates ranged combat, supported by her **harpies**, with the ability to eliminate enemies with a single glance.

**Sinbad the Sailor** accumulates power through travel cards; his abilities grow progressively during the game. He's accompanied by the **Genie**, who provides combat support and strengthens his mobility on the board.

## - Mechanics and Gameplay Experience -

The essence of the game lies in its tactical and highly asymmetric combat: each turn, the player chooses two actions among moving, attacking, or preparing special plays using their hand of cards. The games are swift and strategic, full of critical decisions—should you save cards for defense or use them to attack?

## - Stand-Out Design -

Visual minimalism combined with pre-painted miniatures and Oliver Barrett's art gives personality to each hero and the board, heightening immersion in every game.

## - Why Choose Unmatched: Battle of legends: Volume 1? -

Designed for quick 20-40 minute games, easy to learn but with great tactical depth.

Each hero brings a unique deck, ensuring different experiences in every game.

**Everything is compatible!** All *Unmatched* games and expansions can be combined with each other: you can pit Medusa against Robin Hood, Sinbad against Bigfoot, or take any of the heroes from this box to epic battles on boards from other editions.

Ideal for collectors and players seeking variety and unlimited replayability: the *Unmatched* universe grows constantly, and each new character expands the possibilities for legendary matchups.
